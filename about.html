About Me

Hello and welcome to my fitness blog!

I am Wyatt Oistad. I am a 21 year old male that attends Michigan State University. I am a full time PACS Administrator for MSU Radiology and I take classes part time. In my spare time I love to go to the gym and watch sports. I first started lifting in March of 2021. Since then I have learned a great deal about what it takes to gain strength and muscle.

My Personal Records at 170 pounds:

  • Bench press: 225 pounds
  • Squat: 335 pounds
  • Deadlift: 365 pounds

Here is a picture of when I hit 365 deadlift for the first time

The main purpose of this blog is to spread knowledge around strength, muscle, and dieting. I have learned a lot so far, but I know there is so much more to discover. I hope that my posts will help you achieve your goals and answer all your fitness questions.

Thank you for visiting my blog! I hope you find it informative!
